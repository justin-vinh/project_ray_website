Our Mission

Our Heart & Mission

When a child is in the hospital, their daily life can feel different. Routines may change, and things can feel uncertain or unexpected. Project Ray helps children feel more in control by letting them choose what fun looks like for them.

Children share their ideas by talking, drawing, or playing with modeling dough. Then we work with local makers in Boston to turn those ideas into real toys using 3D printers — quickly and at low cost.

What We Believe

Help children create, play, and feel like themselves — even during a hard time.

  • Every child's imagination deserves to be taken seriously
  • Small, thoughtful innovations make a meaningful difference
  • Community-powered making scales compassion
  • By combining volunteer support, maker spaces, and 3D printing, we transform children's ideas into meaningful, tangible creations
